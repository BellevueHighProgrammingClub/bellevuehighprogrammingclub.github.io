Industry Presentations

Introduction

One of the highlights of Programming Club is the opportunity for club members and CS Students at BHS to hear from industry professionals with multiple years of experiene in STEM fields. From learning about what makes them passionate about their job, to asking questions like daily life at work, this opportunity is an invaluable experience for students who are interested in pursuing a CS or STEM career.
